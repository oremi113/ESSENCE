APP NAME: ESSENCE - Voice Legacy Platform

CORE CONCEPT:
A voice preservation and memory platform that lets users train an AI model on their real voice, then generate new meaningful messages in their voice for loved ones - even after they're gone.

DATABASE STRUCTURE:

Users table:
- user_id
- email
- password (hashed)
- name
- age
- created_date
- voice_model_id (from ElevenLabs)
- voice_training_complete (boolean)

Messages table:
- message_id
- user_id (creator)
- category (children/partner/parents/future_me/family)
- prompt_text
- generated_text
- audio_url (S3 or external storage)
- created_date
- is_private (boolean, default true)
- share_interest_expressed (boolean, for tracking)

Voice_Recordings table:
- recording_id
- user_id
- act_number (1, 2, or 3)
- audio_file_url
- recorded_date
- quality_status
